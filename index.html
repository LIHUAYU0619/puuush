puuush is an experimental platform based on music/sound sharing. Through conversations with people of different identities, we explore the possible modes between working and living. From online listening/communication to offline activity, we hope to build an interdisciplinary cultural space, an open and flexible way of collaboration, and a community where creativity and friendship exist.

puuush是一个基于音乐/声音分享的实验平台，通过与不同身份的个体对话，探索工作与生活的可能性。从线上聆听交流到线下活动，我们希望构建一个界限模糊的跨学科文化场所，一种灵活开放的协作方式，一个创造力与友谊并存的社区。
