Here is a brief introduciton about our project.

puuush is a platform starts from music/sound sharing. It was founded in 2020 by Yue Zhow and June Lihua Yu. Through conversations with people of different identities, we explore the possibility between work and life.


If you are interested in providing...

1. a piece of sound

  • Less than or equal to 1 hour.
  • No restrictions of any kind
    /mix, recorded sound, original music you make, monologue, reading, etc./
  • Optional：a short text along with.
2. the extended work

  • Related to the sound piece, no restriction in media/format.
    /text, poetry, image, performance, video, installation, food, game, etc./
  • It may exist in

    • an html page,
      /is considered as a tiny building, a gallery, a labyrinth, a kitchen, a boat, a gardan, or a basement, etc./
    • a physical object,
      /small production. e.g. one or few pages' prints, letter or postcard, etc. /
    You are choose either or both ways~


Feel free to ask any quesiton via email /puuush.net@gmail.com/, or other ways to find us.
