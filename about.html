puuush is an experimental platform started from music/sound sharing.

Through conversations with people of different identities, we explore the possible modes between working and living. From online communication to offline activity, we hope to build an interdisciplinary cultural space, an open and flexible way of collaboration, and a community where creativity and friendship exist. It is founded in 2020 by Yue Zhow and Lihua Yu.
